Privacy Policy

This page explains how TRANSIT KADIRI respects your privacy and manages information related to Color Sort Shapes Puzzle.

Overview

TRANSIT KADIRI is committed to protecting your privacy. Color Sort Shapes Puzzle is a lightweight puzzle game designed for fun and brain training. This policy reflects the actual behavior of the app and its support channels.

Note: If the app adds features like analytics, ads, cloud saving, accounts, payments, or third-party services that collect data, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Information We Collect

Currently, Color Sort Shapes Puzzle does not require account registration or any sensitive personal information.

  • You can play without signing up or logging in.
  • Game progress, scores, and achievements are stored locally on your device.
  • If you contact TRANSIT KADIRI via email, we may receive the information you provide, such as your email address and your message content.

How We Use Information

  • To provide gameplay features like local progress, high scores, and unlocked content.
  • To respond to your support requests and questions.
  • To improve the game and fix reported issues.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. All gameplay data remains on your device unless a future feature clearly specifies otherwise.

Children's Privacy

Color Sort Shapes Puzzle is suitable for all ages. If we make changes to the game that affect children’s data or content rating, this policy will be updated and reflected on Google Play accordingly.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data in support communications. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact: contact@transitkadiri.site
